Strategy Explanation

This simulator tests a roulette strategy across multiple sessions:

1. Each session starts with your specified starting money

2. Wait until a specific third of numbers hasn't hit for a set number of rounds

3. Start betting on that third with your starting bet amount

4. If you lose, double your bet on the same third in the next round

5. If you win, return to your starting bet and wait for another opportunity

6. A session ends when you either reach the upper border or can't bet anymore

7. Your account balance is tracked across all sessions
