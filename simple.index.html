Strategy Explanation

This simulator tests a roulette strategy where you:

1. Wait until a specific third of numbers (1-12, 13-24, or 25-36) hasn't hit for a set number of rounds

2. Start betting on that third with your starting bet amount

3. If you lose, double your bet on the same third in the next round

4. If you win, return to your starting bet and wait for another opportunity
