Strategy Explanation

This simulator tests a roulette strategy where you:

1. Wait until a specific third of numbers (1-12, 13-24, or 25-36) hasn't hit for a set number of rounds

2. Start betting on that third with your starting bet amount (paid only from starting money)

3. All winnings go to a separate winnings stack

4. If you lose, double your bet on the same third in the next round (from starting money)

5. If you win, return to your starting bet and wait for another opportunity

6. The simulation stops when you reach the money upper border or can no longer bet from starting money
